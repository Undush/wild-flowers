GORGEOUS WILD FLOWERS


Morninig flower


Beneath the crystal clear sky, on lush green hills and valleys grow small light blue flowers with a yellow center. Some call it "Morning flower". In Mongolia, these perennial flowers grow up to 20 cm tall.


Gachuurt. July 2020.


"...The easiest way to grow seedlings and do not forget is to sow the seeds directly in the open ground in July and hide them under cover material for two weeks before they appear. This unpretentious flower prefers light, loose soil - moist, but with good drainage, so the garden should be slightly shaded. Remember, it blooms better in the shade than in the sun, and this process lasts up to 2 months. In addition, in the summer the plants should be fed systematically and regularly, but not watered too much." Source page: Morning flower
