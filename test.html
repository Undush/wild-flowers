Morning flower

"Don't forget me!"

Small light blue flowers with a yellow center on the branch are perennial and annual, with stems 10 to
40 cm tall, surrounded by long, gray-green leaves. Currently, more than 50 memorable names are known,
while the flowers of hybrids can be yellow, pink, cream, purple or white.


The easiest way to grow seedlings and do not forget is to sow the seeds directly in the open ground in July
and hide them under cover material for two weeks before they appear. This unpretentious flower prefers
light, loose soil - moist, but with good drainage, so the garden should be slightly shaded. Remember, it
blooms better in the shade than in the sun, and this process lasts up to 2 months. In addition, in the summer
the plants should be fed systematically and regularly, but not watered too much.

Important! Don't forget me The bush can be divided without fear - the root system of this flower is perfectly suited for transplanting.
